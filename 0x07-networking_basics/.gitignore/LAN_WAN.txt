                    TYPES OF NETWORK 
                    ================


LAN: local area network
WAN: wide area network
WLAN: wireless local area network
MAN: Metropolitan Area Network
SAN: Storage area Network, system area network, server area network, or
sometimes cluster area network
CAN: campus area network, controller area network, or sometimes cluster
area network
PAN: personal area network

LAN: local area network
    connects network devices over a relatively short distance, owned and
controlled by a single person or organisation

WAN: wide area network
    A WAN spans a large physical distance. The internet is the largest
WAN, spannig the earth. Most WAN not owned by a single organisation,
instead exists under collective or distributed ownership and management
